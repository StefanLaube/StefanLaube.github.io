Dr. Stefan Laube

mail AT stefan-laube DOT de

I am a manager at PwC, where I work in the Cyber Security & Privacy department. I help firms develop and implement cyber security strategies, and have a general interest in information systems. Besides, I am a mentor for students studying security management at the International School of IT Security in Bochum.

Previously, I was a post-doc in the Department of Information Systems at Westfälische Wilhelms-Universität Münster, fortunate to be advised by Rainer Böhme.
